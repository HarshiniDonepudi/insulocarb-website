Our Motivation

The journey of Insulocarb began when we witnessed the daily challenges faced by children with Type 1 diabetes. Managing insulin doses, tracking food intake, and predicting glucose responses can be overwhelming, especially for young patients and their families.

Through our partnership with Diabetic Child Society Visakhapatnam, we discovered that many children struggle with maintaining stable blood glucose levels due to the complexity of diabetes management. This inspired us to develop an innovative solution that combines artificial intelligence with augmented reality to simplify and improve diabetes care.

Our mission is to empower children with Type 1 diabetes and their caregivers with intelligent tools that make diabetes management more accurate, less stressful, and more integrated into their daily lives.

Innovative Technology

Insulocarb is pioneering AI-powered nutrition analysis and insulin timing technology specifically designed for Type 1 diabetics. Our comprehensive system combines augmented reality, machine learning, and continuous glucose monitoring to revolutionize diabetes management.

Precise Volume Estimation

Advanced AR technology accurately measures food portions, eliminating guesswork in carbohydrate counting.

Predictive Analysis

AI models analyze meal composition and CGM data to forecast glucose responses with high accuracy.

Intelligent Timing

Smart algorithms recommend optimal insulin dosing times based on individual metabolic patterns.

Our Research Focus

AR Food Analysis

Developing accurate food volume estimation using augmented reality to help children and parents better understand portion sizes.

Glucose Prediction

Creating machine learning models that predict glucose responses in children, accounting for their unique metabolic patterns.

Smart Timing

Optimizing insulin administration timing through advanced algorithms and personalized metabolic pattern analysis.
